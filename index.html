Salads

Veggie noodles

Noodles and veggies that you've left in the fridge. Play with ingredients and add a sweet-sour sauce.

Veggie noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 Broccoli
  • 1 can Mushrooms
  • 1 pack Noodles
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Corn starch
  • 3 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • 4 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Maple syrup

Veggie noodles

Method:

  • Chop the vegetables, carrot, bell pepper, broccoli and mushrooms.
  • Take a pan, pour 3 tbsp of olive oil and let it heat.
  • Put the vegetables in the pan and let them cook until soften.
  • Meanwhile, boil the noodles according package instructions, set aside.
  • Prepare the sauce now, put the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey/maple syrup and the cornstarch.
  • Mix everything back in the pan and let the mixture cook for about 3-5 minutes until the sauce gets thicker.
  • Serve with fresh chopped green onions and sprinkled sesame seeds.
Tofu bowl salad

Fried tofu, avocado, pickled onion, herbs, sesame seeds and a sweet sauce.

Tofu bowl salad

Ingredients:

  • 240g Tofu
  • 1 Avocado
  • 100g Cabbage
  • 1 Red onion
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Salt and pepper
  • 20g Corn starch
  • 3 tsp Rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds

Tofu bowl salad

Method:

  • Prepare the tofu: Chop it in cubes, roll them in a mixture of cornstarch, salt and pepper.
  • Heat up the oil in a pan and fry the cubes until golden brown.
  • Chop the vegetables as you desire.
  • Prepare the sauce made from rice vinegar, soy sauce, honey and a bit of salt.
  • Mix them in a bowl and serve with sesame seeds and sliced hot chilli peppers.
Veggie rice bowl

Rice, roasted tofu and your favourite veggies, seeds and greens. Add soy sauce, tahini and enjoy!

Veggie rice bowl

Ingredients:

  • 240g Rice
  • 1 block Tofu
  • 1 Avocado
  • 100g Red cabbage
  • 0.5 Carrot
  • 1 handful Cashew
  • 10g Sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Tahini
  • 3 tsp Rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 3 tsp Olive oil

Veggie rice bowl

Method:

  • Boil the rice in salted water according to package instructions.
  • Prepare the tofu by chopping it in cubes, roll them in a mixture of cornstarch, salt and pepper.
  • Heat up some oil in a pan and fry the tofu until golden brown.
  • Chop the vegetables as you desire.
  • Prepare the sauce by mixing tahini, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar and sesame seeds.
  • Prepare the plate by putting on a half the rice, on the other half the vegetables and then pour the sauce over and enjoy!

Soups

Parsnip cream soup

A refreshing soup, creamy, delicious that's fits perfect with crunchy croutons.

Parsnip cream soup

Ingredients:

  • 5 Parsnips
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 0.5 Celery
  • 20g Bread
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • a handful Herbs

Parsnip cream soup

Method:

  • Begin by boiling a pot of water and add the salt into it.
  • Add the vegetables into boiling water and check when they are soft enough to put them aside.
  • Meanwhile, prepare the croutons, chop the bread into cubes and fry them in some olive oil until golden brown.
  • Put the boiled veggies into a blender and mix them until it looks like a cream.
  • Pour the soup from the blender into a pot and boil it for 2 minutes.
  • Serve in a large bowl with the crunchy croutons and some of your favourite herbs.
Tomato cream soup

A sweet creamy soup made from juicy tomatoes, fits perfect with roasted seeds and cream.

Tomato cream soup

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Tomatoes
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 Onion
  • 4 pcs Garlic
  • Drizzle on top Sour cream
  • Drizzle on top Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Salt and pepper
  • A handful Herbs

Tomato cream soup

Method:

  • Bring the oven into heat.
  • Grab some fat juicy tomatoes, and the other veggies, rinse them and chop them in large cubes, tomatoes in half.
  • Put the tomatoes into a large tray and sprinkle some olive oil, salt, pepper and dried basil.
  • Roast the veggies until soft.
  • Put the cooked veggies into a blender and blend into a soft cream.
  • Boil the mixture for another 3-5 minutes.
  • Serve into large bowls with sour cream, herbs and croutons.
Veggie cream soup

A healthy veggie soup where you can add really everything and served with croutons.

Veggie cream soup

Ingredients:

  • 10 Tomatoes
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 3 Apio sticks
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 200g Sour cream
  • a handful Herbs
  • 50g Pomegranate seeds

Veggie cream soup

Method:

  • Heat the oven at 200 C.
  • Rinse the vegetables, chop them in large cubes, put them in a large tray, drizzle some olive oil, salt and pepper and let them cook until soften into the oven.
  • Prepare the blender and add the vegetables into it when they're all done, add some water if the soup it's too thick.
  • Pour the mixture into a pot and add the sour cream into it. Boil for about 5 more minutes.
  • Meanwhile, grab some pomegranate seeds.
  • Put the soup into bowls and serve it with herbs, crunchy croutons, pomegranate seeds and some olive oil.

Blueberry muffins

Only a few ingredients like - dates, blueberries, honey, coconut cream, no bake just chill in the fridge for a few hours and voila!

Blueberry muffins

Ingredients:

  • 100g Dates
  • 30g Cocoa
  • 1 cup Oat milk
  • 1 cup Blueberries
  • 50g Dark chocolate
  • 20g Raw almonds
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Blueberry muffins

Method:

  • Put in a blender the dates, cocoa and the oat milk. You need to soak the dates the night before in some warm water so it can become a paste.
  • You will need a muffins tray: portion the mixture for about 1 tbsp in each hole.
  • For the cream you will need the coconut cream and the blueberries.
  • Beat the cream well until it softens and it's thick enough to stay in place. Add the honey.
  • Blend the blueberries and add it in the cream mixture.
  • Pour the cream over the dates base and let the tray cool for about 1 hour.
  • Meanwhile, melt some dark chocolate and draw some lines with it over the little cupcakes, add the almond slices on top and let it cool for another 30 min.
  • Keep them in the fridge for about 3 days and enjoy them cold.
Chocolate sponge

You only need chocolate, flour, vegan plain milk, sugar and vanilla flavour. Bake for 15-20 mins, sprinkle some icing sugar and enjoy.

Chocolate sponge

Ingredients:

  • 400ml Almond milk
  • 4 Eggs
  • 150g Almond flour
  • 200g Dark chocolate
  • 80g Plain sugar
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract
  • 10g Baking powder
  • sprinkle on top Icing sugar

Chocolate sponge

Method:

  • Begin by heating up the oven at 200 C.
  • Melt the chocolate and set aside until it cools down.
  • Beat the eggs whole with the plain sugar until it bubbles on top.
  • Add the almond flour, milk, baking powder, chocolate and the vanilla extract. Mix well but not too hard, so it will remain bubbly.
  • Put the mixture in a tray and bake in the oven for about 30 mins at 180 C.
  • Remove the sponge from the tray and drizzle some icing sugar.
